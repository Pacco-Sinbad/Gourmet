How Do I Purchase?

Currently I am handling all purchases through email correspondence, or at Farmers Markets; look below for my Farmers Market Schedule. I am able to meet with buyers on location by appointment or ship to their residence with shipping fees included. Delivery is a possibility but will incur a fuel charge as well as a %10 fee of the total cost of the order. I accept Paypal, Venmo, and cash for payments.

Bucket Return Discount!

One of my goals is to be as sustainable and environmentally friendly as I can. With that in mind I am offerring a %5 discount on a future purchase for each bucket you return to me up %15 off. If you have more than three buckets, first of all thank you, I can and will accept them but they will not contribute to further percentages off a future order. When making a purchase just inform me you have buckets to return. At this time I am still figuring out how to appropriately reimburse shipping fees for buckets that must be shipped back so stay tuned for an update on that.

Always Available

  • Sterile Bird Seed Spawn Jars $4/$7.50

    I offer jars in pint and quart sizes. These are the exact jars I use. Each jar comes with a fresh air exchange lid and a ring. These jars are not inoculated and you will need a fully colonized jar or live culture to inoculate these ones if purchased. I wll at times have fully colonized spawn jars for sale.

  • Food Grade Mushroom Cultivation Bucket $5

    These are the buckets I use and sell for my ready to grow buckets. I acquire them from a convenience store the previously used them for frsoting. I them clean them and remove the labels. The holes are .75 inches in diameter and spaced to maintain moisture while providing ample space for cluster formation.

  • Pasteurized Straw Bucket (uninoculated) $10

    This is perfect for a grower that has some mushroom spawn and just wants to get bucket or two to experiment with. There may be a two day handling time for me to pasteurize straw in order to fulfill an order for this product but that can be discussed at that time. These buckets are highly reusable!
