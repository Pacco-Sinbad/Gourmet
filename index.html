Fresh Coast Gourmet

Cultivation and Supplier of Premium Mushrooms

What We Do

The goal of Fresh Coast Gourmet is to supply Sheboygan county and the surrounding areas with a fresh supply of premium mushrooms.

I want you to have the best mushrooms possible!

Opperations are based in Cedar Grove, WI. I source as much of my cultivation materials as I can from local sources. This includes straw from local farmers, coffee grounds from local independant shops, and wood chips from local arborists.

Mushroom Varieties

My primary focus is on different types of Oyster Mushrooms

But Why?

Variety

There are an exceptional amount of Oyster Mushroom varieties. My favorite is the Pink Oyster since that is the first mushroom I successfully grew.

Vigor

Oyster Mushroom are a notoriously aggressive cultivar. This means they grow fast and reliably.

Flavor

Many Oyster Mushrooms tend to be subtle but there are differences between the cultivars that provide distinct flavor profiles.

Color

Truly Oyster Mushrooms provide some of the most colorfully pleasing fruits, which makes growing and cooking them exciting!
