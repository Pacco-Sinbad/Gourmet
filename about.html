The goal of Fresh Coast Gourmet is to supply the residents of South East Wisconsin with top quality supplies to cultivate mushrooms at home.

I want you to have the freshest mushrooms possible!

Operations are based in Cedar Grove, WI and performed by owner/operator Mathias Fischer. I prioritize sourcing my cultivation materials from local sources and focus on using recycled materials. This includes straw from local farmers, coffee grounds from local independent shops, and reclaimed food safe containers.

Mushroom Varieties

My primary focus is on different types of Oyster Mushrooms

But Why?

Variety

There is an exceptional amount of Oyster Mushroom varieties. My favorite is the Pink Oyster since that is the first mushroom I successfully grew. This is quickly being beaten out by the flavor and texture of Tarragon Oysters though.

Vigor

Oyster Mushroom are a notoriously aggressive cultivar. This means they grow fast and reliably. Thus, preventing the chances of competing bacteria and fungi to take over a substrate.

Flavor

Many Oyster Mushrooms tend to be subtle but there are differences between the cultivars that provide distinct flavor profiles.

Color

Truly, Oyster Mushrooms provide some of the most colorfully pleasing fruits, which makes growing and cooking them exciting!
